Siargao

Siargao is a world-famous surfing destination known for its powerful Cloud 9 waves, white-sand beaches, and laid-back island lifestyle. It also offers stunning lagoons, rock pools, and island-hopping spots perfect for adventure and relaxation.

4d3n | Php 8,399/pax

Overview

Day 1: Arrival & General Luna Exploration
Arrival at Sayak Airport (10:00 am), and travel to General Luna
Hotel check-in and rest
Visit Cloud 9 (famous boardwalk), explore General Luna area, and optional beginner surfing lessons
Sunset at Catangnan Beach, dinner (clients’ expense), and explore cafes or night market

Day 2: Island Hopping Tour
Breakfast at hotel
Classic Tri-Island tour: Guyam Island (photos and beach time), Daku Island, and Naked Island (white sand views)
Seafood lunch at Daku Island
Sunset by the beach, chill night around General Luna, and dinner (client’s expense)

Day 3: Inland Tour Adventure
Breakfast at hotel and swim at Magpupungko Rock Pools
Lunch at a local restaurant in the Del Carmen/roadside area
Visit Sugba Lagoon (paddleboarding, kayaking, diving platform) optional stop to Coconut Mountain View
Return to General Luna, and dinner (client’s expense) and free time

Day 4: Departure
Slow breakfast, souvenir shopping, and beach walk
Hotel check-out and optional visit to Secret Beach
Transport back to Sayak Airport (11:00 am)
