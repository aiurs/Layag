Coron

Coron is a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear lagoons, dramatic limestone cliffs, and world-famous island-hopping destinations. It is also a top spot for snorkeling, diving, and exploring stunning lakes and hidden beaches.

3d2n | Php 7,699/pax

Overview

Day 1: Arrival & Town Exploration
Arrival at Busuanga Airport (11:00 am)
Land transfer to Coron town
Hotel check-in and rest
Afternoon town tour: Mt. Tapyas viewpoint (sunset viewing) and Maquinit Hot Springs (optional)
Dinner (client’s expense) and free time
Overnight stay at Coron town

Day 2: Island Hopping Adventure
Early breakfast
Full day island hopping tour: Kayangan Lake (famous viewpoint), Twin Lagoon, Barracuda Lake, Smit Beach/CYC Beach, and snorkeling site
Picnic lunch on one of the islands
Return to town after late afternoon
Free time and rest
Dinner (client’s expense) and optional night walk in town

Day 3: Departure
Breakfast at hotel
Free time for souvenir shopping, cafe hopping, or town stroll
Hotel check-out
Transfer back to Busuanga Airport (11:00 am)
