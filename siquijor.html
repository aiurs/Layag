Siquijor

Siquijor is a charming island paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, white-sand beaches, enchanting waterfalls, and mystical folklore. Surrounded by natural beauty and a peaceful atmosphere, it offers the perfect mix of relaxation, adventure, and cultural discovery for every traveler.

3d2n | Php 6,899/pax

Overview

Day 1: Arrival
Arrival at Sibulan Airport (9:00 am)
Land transfer to Dumaguete Port, and ferry to Siquijor
Visit the famous I Love Siquijor Signage, Pitogo Cliff, Balete Tree, Lazi Church and Convent, and Hapitanan
Check-in at the hotel and enjoy free time
Explore the refreshing waters of Cambugahay Falls
Relax and enjoy the sunset at Paliton Beach

Day 2: Full Day Tour
Breakfast at hotel
Full day tour: Lagaan Falls, Lugnason Falls (optional), Sambulawan Underground River, Tubod Marine Sanctuary
Lunch (client's expense)
Enjoy scenic stops at Bucafe / La Canopee and cliff dive at Salagdoong Beach
Return to hotel and rest
Dinner (client's expense) and free time

Day 3: Departure
Breakfast at hotel
Free time for relaxation, exploration and souvenir shopping
Hotel check-out
Ferry back to Dumaguete Port (12:00 pm),
Land transfer to Sibulan Airport and departure
