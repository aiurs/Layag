El Nido

El Nido is a famous tropical destination known for its limestone cliffs, crystal-clear lagoons, white sand beaches, and vibrant marine life. It is a perfect place for island hopping, snorkeling, and relaxing beach adventures.

4d3n | Php 12, 199/pax

Overview

Day 1: Arrival & Town Tour
Arrival at Lio Airport (1:00 pm)
Land transfer to El Nido town
Hotel check-in and rest
Las Cabanas Beach sunset viewing, Corong-corong beach chill, town exploration, and dinner (client’s expense)

Day 2: Island Hopping Tour A
Breakfast at hotel
Full day island hopping tour: Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Secret Lagoon, Shimizu Lagoon, and Seven Commandos Beach
Picnic lunch at one of the islands and return to town after late afternoon
Dinner (client’s expense) and optional night walk in town

Day 3: Island Hopping Tour B
Breakfast at hotel
Full day island hopping tour: Snake Island, Cudugnon Cave, Cathedral Cave, and Pinagbuyutan Island
Lunch during the tour and return to town after late afternoon
Dinner (client’s expense) and optional night walk in town

Day 4: Departure
Breakfast at hotel, and free time for souvenir shopping, café hopping, or beach stroll
Hotel check-out
Transfer back to Lio Airport (12:00 pm)
