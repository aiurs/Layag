La Union

La Union is a vibrant coastal destination known for its surfing spots, beautiful beaches, relaxing atmosphere, and lively food and nightlife scene. With its stunning sunsets, exciting water activities, and growing café culture, it offers the perfect mix of adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable travel experiences.

Meeting point at Manila (12:00 am) and arrival at La Union
Visit heritage sites such as Baluarte Watch Tower, Namacpacan Church of Luna, Bahay na Bato, Paraoir Man-made Forest,

Day 2: Island Hopping Adventure
Breakfast at hotel
Visit Tangadan Falls and explore Imuki Island
Free time for cafe hopping, food trips, and beach activities

Breakfast at hotel
Free time and souvenir shopping

Arrival in Manila after the tour journey
